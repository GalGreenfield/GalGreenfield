SketchUp Redesign Studio

Upload a room, describe the vibe, and get a redesigned SketchUp model.

This demo app accepts a .skp file, runs a mock redesign pipeline, and returns a downloadable SketchUp model.

How it works

  1. Upload an existing SketchUp model of your room.
  2. Describe the redesign goals, style, and must-have elements.
  3. Download the redesigned model and open it in SketchUp.

This prototype is wired to a placeholder redesign pipeline so you can integrate your own model generation workflow later.
